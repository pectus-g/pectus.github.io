Yoru: Eclipse of Two Tails

A narrative action-adventure where every choice bends the world toward Light or Dark. Guide Yoru, a two-tailed nekomata, through Japanese folklore to uncover her unfinished purpose.

Systems

Choose Your Path. Build Your Yoru.

Negotiation & Mercy

Parley with restless souls using dialogue, offerings, and lore-tailored rituals to resolve grudges without violence.

Stylized Combat

Claw, dash, and flame-channel through encounters when you choose retribution. Build combos aligned to Light or Dark rings.

Dual Ability Trees

Two synchronized trees‚Äîone per tail. Mix ring levels to craft hybrid builds and unique traversal/combat synergies.

World-State Quests

Shrines, bosses, and side stories adapt to your moral tilt. The eclipse finale unlocks when all rings are lit.

Tail Rings = World Shift

Raise Light Rings to invite warmth, birdsong, and open paths. Raise Dark Rings to bring moonlight, murmurs, and spectral shortcuts. Hybrid paths unlock secret interactions.
