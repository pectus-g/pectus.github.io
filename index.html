Echoing Tales Between Worlds

In a realm where spirits linger and folklore breathes, ten souls await, each now a monster, each once as innocent as any being born into this world. Loneliness twisted some. Betrayal hardened others. Loss consumed the rest.

What Makes a Monster?

Every yokai you face walks from the pages of Japanese folklore, ancient tales whispered to children as warnings, spirits who once had names. The Oni. The Kitsune. The Yuki Onna. Each was born in legend, shaped by fear and wonder. Each was innocent once, before the world twisted their tales into nightmares. They are monsters now, but does that erase what they were?

Two Paths, No Wrong Answers

Forgiveness is not weakness. Punishment is not cruelty. Each choice demands courage. Each shapes the world around you, turning day to dusk, peace to shadow, warmth to cold.

The Eclipse Awaits

When your tails hold ten souls between them, the Eclipse forms overhead. Few find the balance. Fewer understand what it means. Will you?

Will you remember what they were before the world broke them? Or will you find the strength to end what they have become?

One tail glows with mercy. One tail burns with judgment.
When both hold five souls, the Eclipse reveals itself, and the balance few can bear.

The Heart of Choice

Two Tails. Ten Souls. One Eclipse.

A nekomata's tails are not marks of good and evil, they are mirrors of choice. Your right tail glows when you choose to remember their innocence. Your left tail burns when you choose to end their suffering. Neither path is wrong. Both have power. Both have cost.

Gameplay

Where Folklore Meets Choice

Every system reinforces the duality, your tails mirror the tales you shape

üëπ Ten Yokai, Ten Tales

Ten legendary spirits from Japanese folklore await. Each carries a tale, the Oni's rage from betrayal, the Kitsune's tricks born from loneliness. Begin with dialogue to understand their story, then choose their fate.

‚ú® The Shine System

Shine is your spiritual energy. Powers consume Shine. It regenerates based on your choices: forgiveness regenerates near shrines, punishment through combat. Balance grants steady flow.

üçë Offerings & Sanctuaries

Find shrines to save your journey and restore your spirit. Offer peaches for healing, sake for power. Each shrine reflects your path, glowing with warmth or shrouded in shadow.

‚õ©Ô∏è A World That Remembers

Your choices transform everything: atmosphere, music, NPC reactions. High forgiveness opens peaceful dialogue. High punishment strengthens combat. The world physically shifts with every tale you write.

üåÄ Hidden Depths

Beyond the ten main souls lie side tales, smaller spirits with their own regrets. Each offers glimpses of a deeper story, a shrine, a family, and choices that echo across lifetimes.

üåë The Eclipse Convergence

When all ten rings ignite, an Eclipse forms overhead. This final convergence determines your ending. The Eclipse remembers every tale you've shaped, every choice you've made.

Dynamic World

Where Choices Become Power

This realm breathes with your choices. Walk the path of forgiveness, and the world blooms. Choose punishment, and twilight descends, the same beauty remains, but the air grows heavy with spirits watching from shadows.

Light World

Path of Light

Walk the path of forgiveness, and the world blooms. Autumn trees burn crimson under clear skies, waterfalls sing, the air feels warm and alive. Each soul you persuade strengthens your right tail, granting you deeper understanding, words that heal, and the ability to see beyond the monster.

üåô

Dark World

Path of Shadow

Choose punishment, and twilight descends. The same beauty remains, spirit lanterns glow, waters shimmer; but the air grows heavy. Whispers echo. Spirits watch from shadows. Each soul you end strengthens your left tail, granting you flame, speed, and the power to face what cannot be reasoned with.

üå≥

Ancient Tree

The Eclipse Tree

After each yokai falls, whether through mercy or judgment, you must bring their soul to this ancient tree. Here, beneath its branches, your choice becomes power: A ring ignites on your tail. An ability awakens within you. Persuade a soul, and your right tail glows with wisdom. Punish a soul, and your left tail burns with strength. When you find the balance both tails hold five souls each, the Eclipse forms, and the tree reveals what you truly are.
